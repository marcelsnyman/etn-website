    Landscaping

    About Course:
    This course provides participants with the knowledge to design, create, and maintain gardens and outdoor spaces. It blends creativity with practical skills in horticulture and landscaping
    What You Will Learn:
  • Basics of garden design and layout planning

  • Soil preparation, planting, and irrigation systems
  • Choosing and maintaining plants, trees, and shrubs
  • Pathways, edging, and garden structures
  • Seasonal maintenance and sustainable practices
    • Materials You Will Require:
  • Garden tools (shovel, rake, pruners)

  • Plants, seeds, and soil
  • Fertilizers and mulch
  • Irrigation equipment
  • Landscape design templates
    • Skills You Will Develop:
  • Ability to design aesthetically pleasing outdoor spaces

  • Knowledge of plant care and soil management
  • Installation of irrigation and garden structures
  • Seasonal maintenance techniques
