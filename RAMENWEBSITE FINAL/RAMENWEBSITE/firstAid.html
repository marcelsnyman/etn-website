    About Course:
    This course equips participants with essential first aid skills to handle emergencies until professional medical help arrives.
    What will you Learn:
  • Basic CPR and choking management
  • Wound care, bandaging, and controlling bleeding
  • Managing burns, fractures, and sprains
  • How to respond to allergic reactions, poisoning, and seizures
  • Creating a personal and workplace first aid kit
    • Materials You Will Require:
  • CPR Mannequins
  • Bandages, dressings and antisepetics
  • Splints and slings
  • Latex gloves and masks
  • First aid manual
    • Skills You Will Develop:
  • Confidence in managing medical emergencies
  • Understanding of workplace safety protocols
  • Building and maintaining a first aid kit
