    Garden Maintenace

    About Course:
    Discover the techniques and tools needed to create and maintain a beautiful, thriving garden. This course is perfect for beginners or those looking to enhance their gardening skills, covering everything from planting and pruning to seasonal care
    What You Will Learn:
  • Basics of soil preparation and plant selection

  • Planting techniques for flowers, shrubs, and trees

  • How to maintain lawns, flowerbeds, and vegetable gardens

  • Pest control and disease prevention

  • Seasonal garden care and landscape design tips

    • Materials You Will Require:
  • Gardening tools (trowels, pruning shears, watering cans)

  • Fertilizers and soil conditioners

  • A small garden space or plot to practice hands-on tasks

  • Plants, seeds, or saplings (as specified per lesson)

    • Skills You Will Develop:
  • Ability to plant and maintain a variety of plants and trees

  • Knowledge of irrigation systems and water conservation methods

  • Proficiency in pruning, trimming, and shaping plants

  • Understanding of pest control and environmentally-friendly gardening

  • Landscape design skills for creating a visually appealing garden
