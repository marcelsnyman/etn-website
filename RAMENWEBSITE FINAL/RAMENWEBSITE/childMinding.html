    Child minding

    About Course: This comprehensive course is designed to equip you with the essential skills to provide professional and compassionate child-minding services. Whether you’re new to child care or looking to formalize your skills, you’ll gain practical knowledge in child safety, development, and daily care routine
    What You Will Learn:
  • Understanding child development stages (infants to toddlers)
  • Creating safe, engaging, and educational environments
  • Effective communication with children and parents
  • Managing meals, playtime, and daily routines
  • First aid and emergency response specific to children
    • Materials You Will Require:
  • Child-safe toys and activity materials
  • Basic first-aid supplies/li>
  • A clean, child-friendly space to practice caregiving tasks
  • Course-specific reading materials (provided)
    • Skills You Will Develop:
  • Confidence in caring for children across different age groups
  • Ability to plan age-appropriate activities for cognitive and emotional development
  • Skills in managing challenging behaviors with patience and understanding
  • Knowledge of child nutrition and creating healthy snack plans
  • First-aid response and emergency care for young children
