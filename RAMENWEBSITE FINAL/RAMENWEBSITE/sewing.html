    Sewing

    About Course:
    This course introduces students to the basics of sewing and garment-making. It covers essential skills for creating clothing, accessories, and home décor items
    What You Will Learn:
  • How to use a sewing machine and basic hand-stitching techniques
  • Understanding fabrics and patterns
  • Cutting, pinning, and assembling fabric pieces
  • Creating garments, such as shirts and skirts
  • Repair and alteration techniques
    • Materials You Will Require:
  • Sewing machine
  • Needles, threads, and fabric scissors
  • Measuring tape and fabric chalk
  • Pins, buttons, and zippers
  • Pattern paper and various fabrics
    •
    Skills You Will Develop:
  • Confidence in using a sewing machine
  • Ability to design and construct basic clothing
  • Knowledge of fabric types and sewing tools
  • Basic repair and alteration techniques
