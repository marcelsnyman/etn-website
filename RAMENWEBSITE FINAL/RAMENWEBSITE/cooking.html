    Cooking

    About Course:
    Master the art of cooking through this hands-on, interactive course. Whether you’re a beginner or looking to refine your culinary skills, this course will teach you a wide variety of cooking techniques, flavors, and global cuisines
    What You Will Learn:
  • Basic knife skills and kitchen safety

  • Preparation of various cuisines (Italian, Asian, Mediterranean)

  • How to create a balanced meal using fresh ingredients

  • Food presentation and plating techniques

  • Nutrition basics and how to create healthy meals

    • Materials You Will Require:
  • Basic kitchen tools (knives, cutting boards, pots, and pans)
  • Ingredients for each lesson (provided in the class materials section)

  • A kitchen space to practice and cook your dishes

    • Skills You Will Develop:
  • Understanding of flavour profiles and how to pair ingredients

  • Confidence in preparing meals from scratch

  • Ability to plan and cook meals for various occasions

  • Kitchen management skills, from preparation to cleaning up

  • Enhanced knowledge of nutrition and healthy cooking practices
